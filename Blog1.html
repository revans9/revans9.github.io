Benefits of Green Smoothies

Green smoothies have been on the health scene for a long time, and with good reason.

  • They're packed with vitamins and minerals
  • Increase your consumption of fruit and vegetables
  • Blending the fruit and vegetables retains the fiber, unlike jucing
  • Easy to digest and absorb the nutrients
  • Increased energy
