Acai Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas
  • 1/2 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup cooconut water
  • 1 tbsp acai powder


    • Method

  • Blend the bananas, berries, coconut water and acai powder until smooth
  • Transfer the smoothie to a bowl and add your desired toppings
